ABOUT US

Quintus Investment Company Limited is a private Limited Liability company duly incorporated by the Corporate Affairs Commission (C.A.C) (RC470907) in January 23rd 2003. Its major business involves a wide range of areas such as Engineering Services, Construction, Procurement, Revamping and upgrading of Oil Fields, Instrumentation, Labour Supplies and Miscellaneous Services. The Company offers a platform on which highly professional and experienced group of Engineers, Consultants, and Expatriate in Oil and Gas Services to strive. The Company has also been given the permit by the Federal Ministry of Petroleum Resources to operate as an Oil and Gas Service Company.
